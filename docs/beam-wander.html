Beam Walk Monitoring

Left panels: The walk of the maximum of a 2D fitted Gaussian beam is monitored over time (X-position in the lower subpanel and Y-position in the upper subpanel). This serves as a proxy for resonator cavity deformation, which introduces instrumental effects on the data. Four cameras are currently deployed: IDS00 = horizontal ring, IDS03 = horizontal ring, IDS07 = western ring, IDS05 = northern ring. The last reset marks when the time series was re-zeroed, usually after a manual reorientation of the camera sensor.

Central panels: Spatial trajectory of the fitted beam maximum (X vs Y) illustrating the 2‑D motion of the spot on the sensor.

Right panels: Processing diagnostics generated every 10 minutes showing the 2D Gaussian fit to the camera image; this demonstrates the fitting quality and enables tracking of the beam maximum over time.
