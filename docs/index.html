Seismic Noise Monitoring

Oscilloscope snapshot: Fast check of the operational health of ROMY’s four ring laser gyroscopes (trace numbering/colors):

  • 1 — yellow: horizontal ring
  • 2 — cyan: western ring
  • 3 — purple: northern ring
  • 4 — blue: eastern ring

Ideally, all traces show a sinusoid corresponding to the Sagnac beat note. Contrast (peak‑to‑peak) and DC offset reflect the current instrument settings.
