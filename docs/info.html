ROMY Ring‑Laser Monitor

Site Guide & Documentation

Purpose

This site provides near real‑time and recent historical monitoring products for the multicomponent ring‑laser gyroscope system ROMY (ROtational Motions in seismologY) near Munich, Germany. The focus is operational awareness.

Using the Figures

  • Click any figure to open a larger lightbox view.
  • Images refresh approximately XX min (page specific cadence).

Page Overview

Home
Oscilloscope snapshot of the four ring beat signals and quick-look status context.
Sagnac Spectra
Hourly Sagnac signal power spectral densities per ring with combined multi-ring comparisons for drift assessment.
Beam Wander
Beam walk monitoring from IDS cameras, showing spot trajectories and Gaussian-fit diagnostics.
Beat Drift
14‑day evolution of Sagnac beat frequencies, maintenance / MLTI intervention markers, and MLTI density proxy.
Backscatter
Horizontal ring monobeam diagnostics, backscatter correction performance, amplitude & phase stability indicators.
Environmental
Key environmental drivers (temperature stratification, hydrology/precipitation, tilt, pressure/infrasound, beam position) relevant for drift origin analysis.
Barometric
Focused air pressure & infrasound monitoring (higher temporal detail / filtering variants).
Live Data
Interactive archive browser for daily helicorder and rotation spectra images (last few days per ring).
